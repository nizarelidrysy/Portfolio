;DAMI3000 — Maze Solver


>>github.com/Maze-Solver-Robot

This Arduino-based project is a comprehensive and robust system featuring over 1300+ lines of code and a wide array of functionalities. It incorporates various modes and automated routines, including a detailed Testing mode, Maze mode, Bluetooth mode, Feedback through leds and buzzers... . The project's versatility is further enhanced by its integration of a I2C-LCD, servo motor, 7seg, matrix keypad & an UltraSonic sensor allowing for complex and responsive operations.

This was a PFA(Final Year Project) of the 2nd Preparatory Year, that was graded 17/20 and was rated among the top three projects among all of the classes. More on my LinkedIn/GitHub.
